About Me

Welcome to my page, my name is Arnold Diep. I am currently enrolled at University of Washington Coding Boot Camp program, with an intent of becoming a Full Stack Software Developer. I graduated from the University of Washington with a Bachelor of Science in Environmental Health in 2016. I have 2+ years of experience in Occupational Health and Safety, with my recent employment as a Safety Specialist contractor to Everett Boeing. While working there, I've had the privilege to work with a handful of Service Providers (subcontractors), such as Skanska, Kiewit, VECA, and ChemTreat.

Prior to the bootcamp, I had no coding experience but I have documented my successes and challenges of my journey by taking on weekly application project. I enjoy coding more and more now that have the grasp on the fundamentals. But there were times I felt hopeless because of a mountain of information I accumulated each week and a fast-paced class to cope with. Leveraging from my past experience and with the bootcamp support, I can confidently apply HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap, and JavaScript to create a meaningful, engaging, and impactful experiences for my users.
